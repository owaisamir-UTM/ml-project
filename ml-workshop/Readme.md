** Workshop Goal **

You are given mystery datasets.
Your job is NOT just to code.

Your job is to think like a data scientist:
> Understand the dataset
> Decide the ML problem type
> Identify a target (if any)
> Build a model
> Evaluate and question your results

Files You Will Use
> datasets/ → contains datasets
> ml_starter_notebooks/ → starter notebooks (one per dataset)
This worksheet → your thinking guide

How to Start
> Step 1 — Open Notebook
Open the notebook for your dataset (example):
notebooks/appointments_starter.ipynb

If using Google Colab:
Open from GitHub
Click "Open in Colab"

> Step 2 — Run Initial Cells
DO NOT jump into modeling yet.

> Step 3 — Complete THIS Worksheet First

Note: You MUST complete Part 1 (Problem Understanding)
BEFORE writing any model code.

Rules for This Workshop
- Do NOT:
blindly use all columns
assume the target is obvious
trust high accuracy immediately

- DO:
question your assumptions
discuss with your group
explain your reasoning
